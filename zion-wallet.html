Intro

Blockchain technology is one of the biggest new developments to have hit the technology industry over the last few years. If you are a tech enthusiast, you can see it has become the front and center of the technology discussion. As an one of the most innovative company in the world, HTC would also like to seek some new opportunity by investing in the development and implementation of blockchain technology.

There are many problems to be solved in this concept of smartphone featuring blockchain technology, who is the target user of this product? What impact will it make to the company? What will the final product look like in the market? Thus, I start to cooperate with user research team by using design thinking to solve these problems and try to seek the opportunity of this product.

My Design Process

I decided to follow IDEO’s Human-Centered Design and Lean UX Design Thinking process to make sure that my design decisions were supported by user research and feedback.



User Research

We invite 10 cryptocurrency users who are 25 to 36 year old to participate in this in-depth interviews. The research objective is to understand current cryptocurrency users, including their behaviors, wallet usages, investment behaviors of other assets, current pain points of investment of cryptocurrency, etc. for planning and designing further Exodus products

In general, we found out that most of the participants complained about the mnemonic that help user to restore wallet, they think it’s too long to remember and easy to forget and lose when in need.

Crypto asset trading is the most popular activity in blockchain. Most user regard crypto currency as a investment method, so the security level of wallet is the most important factor considered by cryptocurrency user. In addition, whether the fee in the transaction process can be selected, the transaction speed and status status reminder are the key factors that influence the selection of a service.

Define Problem

Based on the finding from in-depth interview, I designed a Persona to help team more empathetic to identify which problems are the most important to work on solving first.



Then we classify and organize the pain points into similar categories by using affinity mapping.  After discussion, we place the highest priority pain points into the 2 x 2 matrix.

As shown in the 2 x 2 matrix, we can understand the mnemonic is the most important part that contains both the business value and user need. it’s also the key problem that need to be clarified and solved for the following product design.

Pain point: The mnemonic is too long to remember and easy to forget and lose when in need.

In the decentralized world, mnemonic will be the only way to help user restore wallet when they lose phone or forget transaction password. Most users will write it down on the paper (or other things), but these carries are time-limited and there is no guarantee that mnemonic whether complete or not when in need.
