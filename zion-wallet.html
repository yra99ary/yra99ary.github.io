The Problem

Most crypto wallets are in early phases of implementation and adoption, there is still room for improvement when users use these apps. Sending, receiving and storing cryptocurrency can be complicated and confusing, particularly for beginners.

There are many problems to be solved in our first cryptocurrency product with built-in hardware wallet on the phone. Thus, I start to cooperate with user research team by using design thinking to solve these problems we found and try to seek the opportunity of this product.





User Research

We invited 10 cryptocurrency users who are 25 to 36 year old to participate in this in-depth interviews for planning and designing further blockchain products.

The research objective is to understand

• Cryptocurrency user behaviors and wallet usages
• Investment behaviors of digital assets
• Pain points of investment of cryptocurrency





Insights







User Profile

Based on the finding from in-depth interview, I designed a Persona to help team more empathetic to identify which problems are the most important to work on solving first.



Out of all the problems solved I would like to showcase the primary two problems of Recovery key and Sending cryptocurrency.





Problem solved: The recovery key is too easy to forget and lose when in need

We design a new way called “Social Key Backup” to those who accidentally lost wallet or forgot the recovery key, can still restore wallet through people they trust.

Our idea came from the previous in-depth interview, some participants share with us they heard someone splits the recovery key and keep the parts in different locations or trusted people. We found most participants prefer to keep recovery key in trusted people than places. They feel that keep it in people they know is more reliable and difficult to forget.

Basic Social Key Recovery flow:

Iteration:

(BEFORE) Users can't see at a glance the information they need, including the trusted contact list and the backup status of the trusted contact. And users feel confused about if the social backup is enabled or not, the indicator should be more clear.

(AFTER) I combined invitation and list of trusted contact into one page, and made the whole page more clear and readable by using indicators to aware of the current status of backup status.



Solution:

• Social Key Recovery allows users to choose three to five trusted contacts who can help recover their wallet. Once restored, users’ contacts can accept recovery requests to help you recover wallet.

• A status reminder can help users to check contacts from time to time to see if they're still available to help recover wallet.

• If users' trusted contacts copy of recovery data has been lost or corrupted, users can tap Resend by the contact's name to send another copy of recovery data.

• Allow users to update trusted contacts as circumstances require. This might occur if a contact's phone is lost or stolen, or the contact is no longer able to assist in the recovery process





Problem solved: Sending cryptocurrency can be complicated and confusing


Iteration

(BEFORE) We found that users tend to paste address than enter it by themself and they want to make a note for each transaction.

(AFTER) I made some design improvement in the version 2, including added more ways to enter address, emphasized the paste function to replace the traditional input field that user enter the address and added a optional input field for users who want to take memo.

I used Protopie to create two prototypes to determine which one is more effective and have more higher success rate.

Wrap-Up

Zion marks a grand new beginning for HTC, it is the first built-in hardware wallet on the Android phone. We are dedicated to deliver the best user experience through design thinking to help user to reduce the burden of entering cryptocurrency world and come up with iterated solutions for users’ pain points we found from user research.

In our future roadmap, we will keep refining and updating future versions of Zion to make it more better after receiving more user data. And there will be some more advanced functions including partnership with Opera to extend DApp experience, payment, instant exchange and access to wider blockchain marketplace.
