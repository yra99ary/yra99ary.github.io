The Problem

Since the first launch of Edge Sense, we have been continuously monitoring and analyzing the performance of it across the market and community. Most of the feedbacks were positive, but we found the overall rating and sentiment score was gradually decreasing. And some users considered it as a gimmick and limited feature and looked forward to it can be more complete in the future.


User Research

The research aimed to understand the feedback from users with the usage of the current feature. We decided to analyze the user review data from the Play Store and translate them to key factors to determine which part should be improved and what feature to roll out first.
