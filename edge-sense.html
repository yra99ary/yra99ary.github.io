User Profile

Based on the finding from in-depth interview, I designed a Persona to help team more empathetic to identify which problems are the most important to work on solving first.



Then we classified and organized the pain points into similar categories by using affinity mapping. After discussion, we placed the highest priority pain points into the 2 x 2 matrix.



As shown in the 2 x 2 matrix, we can understand the recovery key is the most important part that contains both the business value and user need. it’s also the key problem that need to be clarified and solved for the following product design.

Note: Out of all the problems solved I would like to showcase the primary two problems of Recovery key and Sending cryptocurrency.





Problem solved: The recovery key is too easy to forget and lose when in need

We design a new way called “Social Key Backup” to those who accidentally lost wallet or forgot the recovery key, can still restore wallet through people they trust.

View the original video on official YouTube channel

Our idea came from the previous in-depth interview, some participants share with us they heard someone splits the recovery key and keep the parts in different locations or trusted people. We found most participants prefer to keep recovery key in trusted people than places. They feel that keep it in people they know is more reliable and difficult to forget.



(BEFORE)

Users can't see at a glance the information they need, including the trusted contact list and the backup status of the trusted contact.

And users feel confused about if the social backup is enabled or not, the indicator should be more clear.





(AFTER)

I combined invitation and list of trusted contact into one page, and made the whole page more clear and readable by using indicators to aware of the current status of backup status.



The Solution

• Social Key Recovery allows users to choose three to five trusted contacts who can help recover their wallet. Once restored, users’ contacts can accept recovery requests to help you recover wallet.

• A status reminder can help users to check contacts from time to time to see if they're still available to help recover wallet.

• If users' trusted contacts copy of recovery data has been lost or corrupted, users can tap Resend by the contact's name to send another copy of recovery data.

• Allow users to update trusted contacts as circumstances require. This might occur if a contact's phone is lost or stolen, or the contact is no longer able to assist in the recovery process





Problem solved: Sending cryptocurrency can be complicated and confusing

(BEFORE)

We found that users tend to paste address than enter it by themself and they want to make a note for each transaction.



(AFTER)

I made some design improvement in the version 2, including added more ways to enter address, emphasized the paste function to replace the traditional input field that user enter the address and added a optional input field for users who want to take memo.





The Solution

• We decided to use the current design(scroll panel) which is also has a higher success rate and effective than previous design.

 • Provide more ways to enter address and use an interaction to make transaction process more intuitive and easy to use.

• Transaction fee is adjustable for different needs.
